The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones. It usually starts with 0 and 1, and the next number is found by adding up the two numbers before it.
Formula: F(n) = F(n-1) + F(n-2)
Starting values: F(0) = 0, F(1) = 1
